Benefits of dancing:

  1. a great way to stay fit for all ages, shapes, and sizes;
  2. can meet new friends or even future partners;
  3. increase confidence level, including feeling more comfortable speaking, presenting, and dancing in public;
  4. reduces stress levels, improved relaxation,and making you feel happier after dancing;
  5. helps you set another goal/challenge in life.

Salsa

What is salsa?

Salsa is a dance that has latin and Afro-Carribean roots. A lot of people believe that the basic rythem of salsa was brought by the African slaves during the slave trade. and there are also argument on whether salsa came from Cuba or Puerto Rico.Simiar to Mambo, salsa is also a pattern of six steps danced over eight counts of music.

Merengue

What is Merengue?

Merengue is the national dance of the Dominican Republic and its neighor sharing islands to some extent. There are usually two stories about how the dance came about. One version describes that it is associated with African salves when they were chained together and were foreced to drag one leg as they cut the sugar to the beat of the drum.The ther one is associated with a wounded war -hero's celebration dance,villagers obliged to limp and drag one feet out of sympathy. Merengue music has short and precise rythems and is two even counts and very easy to free style.
